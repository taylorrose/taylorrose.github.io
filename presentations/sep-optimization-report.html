PPC Non-Brand Landing Page Element Testing

• After identifying a lift with the 'Outfiled' page layout, we began testing the individual page elements to understand which combination and organization of elements provided the most optimized experience for users to respond

• Product pannels with videos produced the highest response rate when placed in the first position after the hero section

• A truncated comparison chart followed by a testimonial section performed best in the third and fourth positions on the page

• While removing third and fourth position element decreased response on desktop and tablet traffic, it produced an increased response rate on mobile traffic

• Further testing will confirm if mobile traffic prefers a lighter page with fewer overall elements
