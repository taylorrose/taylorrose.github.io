• Initial call optimization efforts lowered form completions until we found experiences that improved calls without sacrificing forms - Aug 28
• Customer traffic increases factored into the lower response rates during direct to home months
• Google penalty drove less consistent traffic mixes, which effect response rates in Q1 and Q2

2015 Test Goals


Year Total Tests Significant Tests Ratio
YTD 2014 43 18 40%
2015 Goals 58 24 40%


• Increase the total number of tests we launch year-over-year by 35%
• Maintain the ratio of significant tests to total tests
• Optimize for a positive ratio of inbound and outbound leads to best improve overall sales

Cookie Crusade

• Limit noise by only making response decisions on prospect behavior - exclude customers from tests ( 70%+ on home page )
• Cookie after support calls
• Model for Implied Customers
• Email strategies to cookie customers who respond to billing or customer nurture emails

Stark Database

• Own data validity and run queries without affecting site performance
• Integrate real time tracking of test with both form and call success metrics

Granular Segmentation of Success Metrics



• Channel / Source / Creative
• Device Type
• Persona (Cisero)
• Day Parting
• Behavior
• User Type
• Geo
• Cluster

Track Tests as Far Down Funnel as Possible

• Do more leads always mean more sales? Optimize to sales
• Do more sales always mean more revenue? Optimize to revenue

Dynamic Experience For Search Traffic



• Mirror pre-click creative and post-click landing pages
• Adapt pages to search queries and keywords
• Individually optimize and target SEO pages

Optimize beyond entry page



• Multi-page optimization flows
• Drive relevant 'micro' conversions, e.g., video views
• Optimize against scroll depth. Is scrolling the new click?

Expanded LP Portfolio for Affiliates



• Equip affiliates to test their own traffic against an assortment of landing pages they choose from
